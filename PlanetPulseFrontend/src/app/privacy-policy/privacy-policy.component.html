Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PlanetPulse ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our application (PlanetPulse).

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we collect the following information:

User Input Data:

  • Map/City Data: We collect the city you enter for the map display and any associated values you input or select related to that city (e.g., pollution levels, population data). This data is used to display information on the map and perform calculations.
  • Calculator Input: We collect the values you enter into the CO2 calculator to provide you with results.

Account Information:

  • Profile Picture: If you choose to upload a profile picture, we store it as part of your user profile.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: We use your profile picture for your user profile.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Specifically:

  • Password Security: Your passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing algorithms to protect them.
  • Data Retention: We retain your user input data (map/city data and calculator input) as long as your account is active. You have the ability to clear your history within the app.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all of your data, including your profile picture and user input history, will be permanently deleted from our systems.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the App (e.g., hosting providers). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Clear History: You can clear your map/city data and calculator input history within the App.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will result in the deletion of all your data.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App.
